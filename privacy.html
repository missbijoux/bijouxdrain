bijouxdrain — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

bijouxdrain is a browser extension that adds a decorative, themed overlay (floating images and a gift menu) on top of the creator's page at throne.com/bijoux. We take a minimal-data approach. This policy explains exactly what the extension does and does not do with information.

Information we do NOT collect

bijouxdrain does not collect, store, sell, or transmit any personal information. It has no analytics, no advertising, no trackers, and no accounts. It does not read, record, or transmit anything you type or do on Throne, and it never accesses your payment details. All purchases are completed by you, directly on Throne's own pages.

Information stored locally on your device

The extension saves two small preferences in your browser's local storage, purely so the experience behaves nicely. These never leave your device:

  • that you acknowledged the 18+/consent notice (so it isn't shown repeatedly), and
  • where you dragged the overlay panel (so it stays put).

Network requests

To display its decorative images, the extension requests images from the creator's own image service hosted on Railway (*.up.railway.app). This is a standard web request to load images; like any web request it includes your device's IP address and user-agent, which we do not log, store, or use to identify you.

Where it runs

The extension only activates on https://throne.com/bijoux. It does not run on any other website.

Permissions

  • Host access to throne.com — to display the overlay on the creator's Throne page.
  • Host access to *.up.railway.app — to load the decorative images.

Children

bijouxdrain is intended for adults (18+) only and is not directed to children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly.
