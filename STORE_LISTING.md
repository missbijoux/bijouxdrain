## Detailed description

bijouxdrain adds a playful, Bijoux-branded overlay on top of the creator's page
at throne.com/bijoux. When you open that page, soft floating images drift over it
and a small movable panel shows Bijoux's gift menu and a running tally of gifts
you've chosen to send.

It's designed for adult fans (18+) who enjoy a fun, immersive way to support the
creator — with you fully in control the entire time:

• One-tap gifting — tapping a gift opens that item on Throne, where YOU complete
  the real purchase yourself. Nothing is ever charged automatically.
• Always in control — a visible close button turns the overlay off instantly, and
  closing the tab ends everything. Nothing is blocked or trapped.
• Ambient only — the floating images are click-through and never cover or interfere
  with Throne's own buttons.
• Runs only on throne.com/bijoux — nowhere else.

bijouxdrain does not collect personal data, has no ads or trackers, and never
touches your payment information. See the privacy policy for details.

This is a fan experience intended for adults.
